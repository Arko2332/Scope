QUESTIONS

No BS answers.

Is it actually free?
Yes. The scanner is free. You enter your email, you get instant access. We'll also send you a weekly trend briefing, but you can unsubscribe anytime. No credit card, no hidden fees, no catch.
What platforms does it pull data from?
The scanner cross-references sold prices, listing counts, and sell-through velocity from StockX, eBay, and Grailed. On top of that, we track social signals from Instagram and TikTok, celebrity sightings, Reddit community sentiment, Google search trends, and supply levels across platforms — all factored into every score.
How accurate are the recommendations?
The scanner analyzes real market signals — not gut feelings. That said, it's a tool to support your decisions, not replace them. The data shows you where momentum is building and where it's fading. What you do with that information is up to you.
Is this for new releases or vintage only?
Vintage only. The scanner tracks 166 vintage models from the '60s through the 2010s — Jordans, Dunks, Air Max, LeBrons, Reeboks, New Balance, Adidas, and more. If you're flipping hyped new releases, this isn't built for you.
What if the shoe I'm looking for isn't in the database?
You can request any model to be added. We'll start tracking it and notify you when it's ready to scan with full market data. Your requests also help us prioritize which models to add next.
What's the difference between this and Scope Pro?
The free scanner lets you scan up to 8 pairs at a time with snapshot data. Scope Pro tracks 14,000+ models continuously, sends real-time alerts when pairs drop below trend price, and gives you a 48-hour head start before the market corrects. Early access coming soon.
